Coloring Bunworld

Helo! This is Maggie, aka lil Baker.

  1. Put a page of Bunworld upsidedown on your light box. Set another sheet of copy paper on top and trace the original page. Now, set the copy aside.
  2. Using a light box and tracing paper, again trace Bunworld backwards, but this time, make individual tracings of the different colors.
  3. Transfer the tracings onto craft foam with a pencil by rubbing with a pencil or a bone folder using pressure.
